What is Joystick Jury?

Joystick Jury is a platform where college students can share their video game reviews, discover new games, connect over shared interests, and build friendships in a digital space.

Do I need to create an account to use this site?

No. You may freely browse all of Joystick Jury without an account. But, if you want to record or review any games, or otherwise participate, you will need to create one.

Is there any cost to join Joystick Jury?

No, Joystick Jury is free for all college students. We believe in creating an accessible community where everyone can share their passion for gaming.

How can I find and join communities?

Easily browse communities based on game genres, specific games, or college groups. Join any that interest you to start engaging with other members.

What makes Joystick Jury unique?

Aside from focusing solely on video games, our platform is built specifically for college students looking to form meaningful connections through gaming.
